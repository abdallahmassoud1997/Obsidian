## 2. Course Information

### A) Overview

The Introductory Dive is the perfect first step into the underwater world for beginners with no previous scuba diving experience. This discovery dive allows you to experience the beauty of the Red Sea safely under the close supervision of a professional instructor. You will learn the basics of scuba diving, practice essential skills, and enjoy your first underwater adventure on calm and beginner-friendly coral reefs. The experience includes a full equipment setup, safety briefing, surface practice, and the chance to explore vibrant marine life at depths of 5–7 meters.

### B) What You Will Learn:

- How to use scuba diving equipment
- Basic breathing techniques underwater
- Buoyancy control and body movement
- Diving safety procedures
- Underwater communication signals
- How to enjoy your first dive confidently
- How to explore coral reefs safely

### C) Course Schedule

#### Arrival & Briefing

- Board the dive yacht
- Receive a safety and diving briefing
- Learn basic underwater skills

#### Equipment & Practice

- Get fitted with diving gear
- Practise breathing and buoyancy on the surface

#### First Dive

- Explore a beginner-friendly Red Sea reef
- Dive to **5–7 meters** for **20–30 minutes**
- Discover corals and tropical fish

#### Relax on Board

- Enjoy lunch and relax on the yacht
- Snorkel or prepare for the next dive

#### Second Dive & Return

- Join a second discovery dive at another reef
- Relax on the way back and review dive photos
