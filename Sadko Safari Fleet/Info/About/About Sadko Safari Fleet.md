About Sadko Safari Fleet:
Sadko Safari Fleet is a leading diving center in the Red Sea, Egypt, founded in 1999 and specializing in liveaboard diving safaris, daily diving trips, private yacht charters, and scuba diving courses. With a fleet of dedicated safari yachts, including MV Sunshine, MV Sunlight, and MV Springland, Sadko provides comfortable and safe access to world-class dive sites across the Red Sea. Sadko aims to deliver unforgettable underwater experiences through professional guidance, high-quality service, and a passion for exploring and protecting the marine environment.

### Business Goals
#### Services:
1. **Liveaboard safaris:**
Sadko offers Red Sea liveaboard diving safaris aboard its dedicated yachts with more than 15 custom itineraries. Divers can explore world-class reefs, wrecks, and remote dive sites across the Red Sea while enjoying comfortable accommodation, professional dive support, and a personalized safari experience.

2. **Daily diving trips:**
Sadko provides daily diving trips from Hurghada, offering certified divers the opportunity to explore nearby Red Sea dive sites. Trips include boat transfers, dive guidance, and organized schedules suitable for different experience levels.

3. **Diving courses:**
Sadko Safari Fleet offers NDL (National Dive League) certified diving courses designed for beginners, certified divers, and professionals. With a range of 9 diving courses, Sadko helps divers develop their skills, improve their underwater confidence, and advance their certifications through professional instruction and practical training.

4. **Private charters for (clubs, agencies, groups):**
Sadko provides private yacht charters for diving clubs, travel agencies, kiteboarding clubs, and groups looking for customized Red Sea experiences. Charter trips can be tailored with flexible itineraries, exclusive routes, and dedicated services.

5. **Long-Range Trips & Overnight Adventures**:
For divers seeking more exclusive experiences beyond regular daily diving, Sadko offers long-range day trips and overnight adventures to remote Red Sea dive sites. These trips provide access to famous locations such as Abu Nuhas, SS Thistlegorm, and Salem Express, with extended dive time, night dives, and the comfort of a safari yacht experience.

6. **Dive equipment sales:**
Sadko offers a selection of diving equipment and accessories for divers, providing essential gear from trusted brands to support safe and comfortable underwater adventures.

#### Fleet Summary
1. [MV Sunshine]([MV Sunshine liveaboard Red Sea with Sadko Safari Fleet](https://www.sadko-safarifleet.com/boats/sunshine))
MV Sunshine is Sadko Safari Fleet’s first liveaboard yacht, launched in 2013. Designed for comfortable Red Sea diving safaris, it offers spacious cabins, modern facilities, and dedicated diving areas. With a 31 m length and 7.5 m beam, the yacht accommodates up to 20 guests in 10 cabins, providing a relaxed and social atmosphere for divers exploring the Red Sea.

2. [MV Sunlight](https://www.sadko-safarifleet.com/boats/sunlight)
MV Sunlight is a modern liveaboard yacht launched in 2017, built for extended Red Sea diving expeditions and southern routes. Featuring comfortable accommodation, professional diving facilities, and spacious relaxation areas, it measures 34 m in length and 7.25 m in beam. The yacht hosts up to 20 guests across 10 cabins, combining comfort, safety, and adventure.

3. [MV Springland](https://www.sadko-safarifleet.com/boats/springland)
MV Springland is Sadko Safari Fleet’s high-speed safari yacht, introduced in 2022. Designed for daily diving trips, mini-safaris, and full-week adventures, its speed allows divers to reach dive sites earlier and enjoy quieter underwater experiences. With a 31 m length and 7.25 m beam, it accommodates 20 guests in 10 cabins while offering modern diving equipment and comfortable onboard spaces.
