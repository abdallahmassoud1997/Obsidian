# Company Brief

ROI Real Estate is an Egyptian real estate brokerage company based in Hurghada, specializing in residential and commercial properties across the Red Sea region. The company offers end-to-end real estate solutions including buying, selling, renting, property management, investment consultancy, maintenance, interior design, construction, and legal assistance. ROI partners with trusted developers and focuses on delivering professional, transparent, and investment-oriented services for both local and international clients.

# Business Goals
- Generate qualified real estate leads.  
- Increase property sales and rentals.  
- Position ROI as a trusted real estate brand in Hurghada and the Red Sea.  
- Help clients maximize their return on investment (ROI).  
- Expand partnerships with leading developers.
